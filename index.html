Synopsis

A surprise attack in outer space forces the Enterprise to crash-land on a mysterious world. The assault came from Krall (Idris Elba), a lizard-like dictator who derives his energy by sucking the life out of his victims. Krall needs an ancient and valuable artifact that's aboard the badly damaged starship. Left stranded in a rugged wilderness, Kirk (Chris Pine), Spock (Zachary Quinto) and the rest of the crew must now battle a deadly alien race while trying to find a way off their hostile planet.

The Cast

Chris Pine as James T Kirk

James Tiberius Kirk was a 23rd century Human Federation Starfleet officer. As a Starfleet cadet, he was instrumental in the defeat and death of Nero, a Romulan bent on the obliteration of the entire United Federation of Planets. As a result, he was commissioned directly to the rank of captain and appointed as commanding officer of the service's flagship, the USS Enterprise.


Simon Pegg as Montgomery Scotty

Montgomery Christopher Jorgensen Scott was a 23rd century Human individual who served in the Federation Starfleet as an engineer, in the milieu of the Kelvin timeline created by Nero's time travel to the year 2233. After a mishap with a transporter, Scotty was sent to a mundane assignment as a punitive measure, but after helping James T. Kirk escape from Delta Vega, Scotty was rewarded with a position as chief engineer of the USS Enterprise.

Zachary Quinto as Spock

S'chn T'gai Spock was a half-Vulcan/half-Human Starfleet officer who served the Federation in the 23rd century, best known as the first officer and science officer of the Federation starship USS Enterprise.

Zoe Saldana as Uhura

Nyota Uhura was a Human female who served as the communications officer on the Federation starship USS Enterprise from 2258 to at least 2263, when the Enterprise was destroyed in orbit of Altamid.

Karl Urban as Leonard Mccoy

Leonard Horatio McCoy was a 23rd century Human man, a Starfleet doctor. From the year 2258 to the five-year mission that began in 2260, McCoy was chief medical officer of the Federation starship USS Enterprise.

Anton Yelchin as Pavel Chekov

Chekov was born in 2241 in Tagnarog, Russia on Earth but spent a few years at the Luna colony of Mare Imbrium. Chekov was an only child. He attended a series of elite Russian and international schools, ultimately entering Moscow State University, from which he graduated in 2256. The same year, he enrolled in Starfleet Academy, becoming the second-youngest freshman in the Academy's history.

John Cho as Hikaru Sulu

Hikaru Kato Sulu was a 23rd century Human male Starfleet officer in the Kelvin timeline created by Nero's time travel. He served as the helmsman aboard the USS Enterprise.
